Our Two Cents

About Us

Our Two Cents is a blend of two great minds, personalities and friends who strongly believe in th power of words. Our mission is to leave footprints in a universe so wide we cannot fathom. In short, we are here to let you know that you matter, everyone does.

The universe is full of people from all walks of life and that's what makes it beautiful. To think that in a room of 35 people there exists 35 mindsets, 35 paradigms and 35 different human beings. Again I say, that's what makes it beautiful.

The essence of Our Two Cents is to add our thoughts on things that matter, things that build and things that edify others. Our desire is to make significant impact in the lives of others.


Our Objectives

  • To significantly impact lives.
  • To write literature that edifies others.
  • To be authentic and write about our thoughts on various themes.
